Scoring (last 30 days)

  • V30 — total views in your season playlist
  • L30 — total likes in your season playlist
  • ER — engagement rate = L30 / max(V30,1), cap 5%

Robust scaling to reduce outliers:

zV = (V30 − median(V30)) / IQR(V30)
zL = (L30 − median(L30)) / IQR(L30)

Score: 0.5·zV + 0.3·zL + 0.2·norm(ER)

Weights and caps may be tuned during pilot seasons.
