Full Rules — terms of participation (MVP/experimental)

1. General

  • These Rules govern participation in the “AI Games Challenge” (the “Challenge”).
  • The Challenge is currently an MVP / experimental community project with no guaranteed prizes or obligations.
  • By registering, you agree to these Rules and the policies referenced herein.

2. Eligibility

  • Individuals and teams with a public YouTube channel may participate.
  • You must comply with laws of your jurisdiction. If you are a minor, ensure participation is permitted by local law and your guardian.
  • You confirm you have the rights to all submitted materials (videos, images, texts, logos).

3. Registration & Verification

  • Register via the official Join page and provide team name, region, contact, channel URL, and season playlist URL.
  • You will receive a unique verification token. Paste it into your playlist description. The token is used to verify channel ownership.
  • Submissions may be moderated or declined if incomplete, unlawful, or violating platform guidelines.

4. Season Structure

  • The season consists of: Qualifiers → Quarterfinals → Semifinals → Finals.
  • Announcements and stage tasks are posted on the official AI Games Challenge YouTube channel.
  • Details of the stage mechanics are described on the Rules (overview) page.

5. Scoring & Results

  • Qualifiers & Quarterfinals: ranking uses YouTube metrics from your season playlist (e.g., views, likes, engagement).
  • Semifinals & Finals: ranking is a mix of YouTube metrics and jury scoring (creativity, clarity, team effort).
  • Organizers may remove illegitimate activity (e.g., bot traffic) from scoring and may disqualify for manipulation.
  • Results are published on the website/official channels. Organizer decisions are final for the MVP phase.

6. Content Rights & License

  • You retain all copyrights to your materials.
  • By participating, you grant the Organizer a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, display, and share your submitted/public Challenge materials (videos, images, logos, rules) solely for promotion, news/updates, and documentation of the Challenge.
  • You confirm your materials do not infringe third-party rights and comply with YouTube Community Guidelines.

7. Safety & Fair Play

  • No dangerous stunts; respect people, property, and local regulations. Play responsibly and at your own risk.
  • Anti-gaming: artificial boosting (bots, fake views/engagement, misleading practices) is prohibited and may lead to disqualification.
  • Organizer may request evidence of fair play or remove content that is unsafe, illegal, or otherwise inappropriate.

8. Data & Privacy

  • We collect team/contacts and public links you provide for the purpose of running the Challenge.
  • Personal data will not be sold or shared with third parties except as required by law or for essential service providers (e.g., GitHub/Google for hosting).
  • You may request removal of your registration by contacting us via the official channels listed on Contact.

  • The Organizer provides the Challenge “as is” during the MVP/experimental phase.
  • The Organizer is not liable for platform outages (YouTube, GitHub, etc.), network issues, data loss, or any injury or damage arising from participant activities.
  • Participation is voluntary; you are responsible for your own actions, health, and equipment.

10. Prizes & Consideration

  • During the MVP phase, there are no guaranteed prizes. Any future rewards/sponsorships will be announced separately with specific terms.

11. Disqualification & Enforcement

  • The Organizer may warn, limit, or disqualify participants for violations of these Rules or platform policies.
  • In clear cases of fraud, manipulation, or rights violations, submissions may be removed from public listings and leaderboards.

12. Changes & Governing Terms

  • These Rules may be updated; changes will be published on the website or official channel.
  • Where applicable, platform terms (YouTube, GitHub) prevail over conflicting provisions here.

13. Contact

  • Partnership proposals: use our Telegram partnerships bot listed on the Contact page.
