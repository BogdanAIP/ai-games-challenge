Vortex (AI-generated, 2025)

Hybrid of football, handball and basketball. Three field zones with distinct control rules.

Vortex Logo

Rules

Field: Indoor 40×20 m, goal 3×2 m, ball size 4–5. Three zones: Defense / Turbo Center / Attack.

  • Defense: hands only (like handball).
  • Turbo Center: any control, max 3 seconds.
  • Attack: feet only; goals count only from a foot shot in Attack zone.

Scoring: Goal = 1 point. Optional “golden pass” = 2 (experimental).

Fouls: no holding/tackles; 5 personals → 2 min penalty.

Timing: 3×10 min, rolling substitutions.

Street Ball Mix (AI-generated, 2025)

Street basketball with progressive modes: Classic → “33” → “Sniper”.

Street Ball Mix Logo

Rules

Format: 1×1, 2×2, 3×3. Best of 3 sets to 15.

  • 0–7: Classic streetball rules.
  • 7–12 (“33”): ≥3 dribbles before shot; 3-pointer = +1 bonus.
  • 12–15 (“Sniper”): call the shot type; success = double points.

Extras: dunk attempt +1; 14–14 → 3-shot shootout without defense.
