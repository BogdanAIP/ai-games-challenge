Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join?

Anyone. Solo or team. You only need a public YouTube channel and a season playlist.

Do I need a new channel?

No. Use your existing channel with a dedicated season playlist.

Which metrics are counted?

Views and likes from videos in your season playlist; engagement rate is capped. See Scoring.

Can I post on TikTok/IG?

Yes. All social is welcome, but the leaderboard uses only your YouTube season playlist.

How do you prevent cheating?

We only count post-registration uploads in your public playlist, cap engagement, and review suspicious patterns.
