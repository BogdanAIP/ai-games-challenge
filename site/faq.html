Quick facts

Who can join?

Anyone. Solo or team. You only need a public YouTube channel and a season playlist.

Do I need a new channel?

No. You can use your existing channel and add a dedicated season playlist.

Which metrics are counted?

Views and likes from videos in your season playlist; ER is capped. See Leaderboard & Scoring.

Other social apps?

Use any social platform to grow your audience, but the leaderboard relies on YouTube playlist only.

Safety

Please play responsibly and follow local laws. See Full Rules.
