Participation

  1. Invent a hybrid sport (AI tools allowed).
  2. Create a public YouTube playlist named “AI Games Challenge — Season 1”.
  3. Add our verification token to the playlist description (from your profile after registration).
  4. Upload match videos to that playlist during the season window.

Content & safety

  • Rules must be clear and repeatable for others.
  • No dangerous stunts; use appropriate locations/gear.
  • Respect privacy, local laws, and property.
  • Use music/footage you are allowed to use.

Fair play

  • We count only videos in your season playlist.
  • Only videos published after registration.
  • Engagement rate is capped; suspicious spikes may be reviewed.
