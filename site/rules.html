Participation

  1. Invent a hybrid sport (AI tools allowed).
  2. Create a public YouTube playlist named “AI Games Challenge — Season 1”.
  3. Register your playlist on our site (coming soon) and add the verification token to the playlist description.
  4. Upload match videos to that playlist during the season window.

Content guidelines

  • Keep rules understandable and repeatable for others.
  • No dangerous stunts; use appropriate locations and gear.
  • Respect privacy and local regulations where you film.
  • Music/footage must be licensed for your use.

Fair play / anti-gaming

  • Only videos in the season playlist are counted.
  • Only videos published after registration are counted.
  • Engagement rate is capped; suspicious spikes may be reviewed.

We may adjust rules between seasons for safety and fairness.
