AI Games Challenge

Get started in minutes

  1. Register with Google and link your YouTube channel.
  2. Create a new hybrid using AI (e.g., football + volleyball + tag — or any mix).
  3. Play & film: record how you or your team play the new game.
  4. Publish the video(s) to your public YouTube season playlist.
  5. Climb the leaderboard — updated daily from YouTube metrics.
